Atoms in a Grid

Interaction

Left-drag an atom to deform the lattice. Shift-click an atom to add or remove a fixed point. Shift-drag a fixed point to move it. Right-drag or middle-drag to rotate, including while an atom is being dragged. Use W/A/S/D to pan and the mouse wheel to zoom.

Display

Tubes show bond strain: warm means stretched, cool means compressed. Atoms show accumulated local energy more softly.
